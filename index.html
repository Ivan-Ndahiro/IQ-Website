Welcome!

Hello, this website is part of a study for a final year project at the University of York.

Here we will have an IQ test that will hopefully challenge you but don't worry, no personal information will be recorded. It incorporates a mix sequential and odd-one-out puzzles.

Please head to the Information page for further details regarding the history and use of IQ tests.

Rules:

1. You must answer all 30 questions within the 25 min timer.

2. You must participate without any help from resources or other people.
